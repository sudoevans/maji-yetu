Turning the Tide: Uniting for a Drought-Resilient Future!


At MajiYetu, we believe that water is life. We are a non-profit organization that works with communities in Kenya to provide access to clean water and sanitation. Our mission is to empower communities to build sustainable water systems that will improve their health and livelihoods.

More About Water Crisis

Climate change effects on Drougt


"Climate change intensifies drought, disrupting ecosystems, agriculture, and communities worldwide. Rising temperatures escalate evaporation, parching lands, and depleting water sources. Erratic weather patterns exacerbate dry spells, compounding agricultural challenges and threatening food security. The cumulative impact amplifies socio-economic disparities, prompting urgent action to mitigate the growing menace of prolonged droughts and their far-reaching consequences on our planet.".
